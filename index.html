Hello there!

AccessYourCoast has been constructed and is maintained by the California Coastal Commission. The data is a publicly available open data set of all California Coastal Access locations and their respective attributes, hosted by Google FusionTables.

Location and attribute data are collected primarily from entries in the statewide California Coastal Access Guide: Seventh Edition (2014), and the 4-book set of regional Experience the California Coast guides (2005, 2007, 2009, 2012), all published by the University of California Press.
